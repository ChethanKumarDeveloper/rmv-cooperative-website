Member Services

Financial Services

RMV Credit Cooperative Society provides financial services exclusively to its members in accordance with its registered bylaws and cooperative principles.

Savings Facilities

The society offers savings-oriented deposit facilities such as savings accounts, fixed deposits, recurring deposits, and other approved schemes for members.

Credit Facilities

Members may avail credit facilities including personal loans, business or self-employment loans, emergency loans, and secured loans, subject to eligibility and approval.

Loan Approval Process

Loan approvals are based on membership status, shareholding, repayment capacity, and policies approved by the managing committee.

Service Disclaimer

All services are provided only to members and are subject to society bylaws, internal policies, and applicable cooperative laws.
