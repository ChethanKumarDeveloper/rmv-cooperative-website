Governance

Democratic Management

RMV Credit Cooperative Society follows a democratic governance structure where members collectively participate in decision-making through elected representatives.

General Body

The General Body, consisting of all members, is the supreme authority of the society. Major decisions are taken through resolutions passed in General Body Meetings.

Managing Committee

The Managing Committee or Board of Directors is elected by the members to oversee day-to-day administration and policy execution.

Office Bearers

Office bearers such as the President, Vice-President, Secretary, and Treasurer are appointed as per the society’s bylaws.

Annual General Meeting

The Annual General Meeting (AGM) reviews accounts, approves audits, declares dividends if applicable, and discusses policy matters.
