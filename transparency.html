Transparency

Compliance & Legal Framework

RMV Credit Cooperative Society operates strictly in accordance with the applicable Cooperative Societies Act and is subject to oversight by the Registrar of Cooperative Societies.

Registration & Oversight

The society is duly registered under the Cooperative Societies Act and functions under the supervision of the Registrar of Cooperative Societies.

Audit & Records

Statutory records are maintained as prescribed, and annual audits are conducted in compliance with cooperative regulations.

Returns & Filings

Required returns and reports are filed with the competent authority within the stipulated timelines.

Important Disclosure

RMV Credit Cooperative Society is not a bank and is not regulated by the Reserve Bank of India. Services are provided exclusively to members.
