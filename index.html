Member-Owned Cooperative Society

Empowering Members Through Collective Financial Strength

RMV Credit Cooperative Society is a member-driven financial cooperative committed to responsible savings, mutual support, and accessible credit, operating strictly under cooperative principles.

Become a Member

Member Services

The society provides essential financial services exclusively to its members, guided by cooperative principles and approved bylaws.

Savings Facilities

Members are encouraged to build disciplined savings through savings accounts, fixed deposits, recurring deposits, and other approved schemes.

Credit Facilities

The society provides credit support to eligible members in the form of personal loans, business loans, emergency loans, and secured loans, subject to approval.

Our Cooperative Values

The society is guided by core cooperative principles that ensure fairness, transparency, and long-term member welfare.

Democratic Control
One-member-one-vote system ensuring equal participation.
Member Welfare
Policies designed to benefit members, not external entities.
Transparency
Statutory audits and disclosures as per cooperative laws.
Financial Inclusion
Promoting responsible savings and accessible credit.
Community Development
Collective growth for the member community.

RMV Credit Cooperative Society is a member-owned cooperative society. It is not a bank and is not regulated by the Reserve Bank of India. Services are available only to members and are governed by registered bylaws.
