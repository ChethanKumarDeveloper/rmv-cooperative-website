Membership

Becoming a Member

Membership in RMV Credit Cooperative Society is open to eligible individuals as defined in the society’s registered bylaws. Members are both owners and beneficiaries of the cooperative.

Membership Features

Members are required to purchase the minimum share capital as prescribed. Each member has equal voting rights regardless of the number of shares held.

Member Rights

Members have the right to vote, attend general body meetings, inspect records as per bylaws, and receive dividends if declared.

Member Responsibilities

Members are expected to follow the society’s bylaws, maintain financial discipline when availing services, and act in the interest of the cooperative.

Important Note

Membership does not require mandatory use of financial services. Members may hold shares only and still remain valid members.
