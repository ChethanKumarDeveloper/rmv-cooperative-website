About the Society

Who We Are

RMV Credit Cooperative Society is a member-owned cooperative financial institution established to promote mutual financial support, savings discipline, and affordable access to credit among its members.

The society operates on cooperative principles where members are both owners and beneficiaries. It exists to serve member interests rather than maximize profits.

Our Purpose

To strengthen financial inclusion and collective growth through responsible savings and credit practices.

Our Values

Mutual trust, democratic control, transparency, member welfare, and community development.
